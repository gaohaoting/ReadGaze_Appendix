# Anonymous GitHub mirror (anonymous.4open.science)

1. Create a **new public GitHub repository** with a **neutral name** (no lab name, no paper title substring that is unique to your group).
2. Push this repository (see `GITHUB_SETUP.md`).
3. Open [https://anonymous.4open.science/](https://anonymous.4open.science/) and follow the site instructions to register your GitHub repository URL.
4. Copy the generated **anonymous URL** and use it in the paper appendix only.
5. After the review period, follow the site policy for updating or removing the anonymous clone.

Do not share the mapping between the anonymous URL and your real identity in public channels during review.
